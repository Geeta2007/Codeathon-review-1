About BMSCE

One of India's Oldest & Finest Engineering Colleges

BMS College of Engineering (BMSCE), founded in 1946 by Late Sri. Bhusanayana Mukundadas Sreenivasaiah, is the first private engineering college in pre-independent India. Located on Bull Temple Road, Basavanagudi, Bengaluru, it is affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU) and has been autonomous since 2008.

The college is accredited by NAAC with A++ grade (CGPA 3.83/4 — highest in Karnataka), and most programs are accredited by NBA. It is recognised by UGC and approved by AICTE.

BMSCE has produced over 40,000 engineers and leaders who have made significant contributions worldwide. The institution offers 38 programs at UG, PG, and PhD levels, and houses 14 research centres on campus with 350+ PhD scholars.

The Training & Placement Cell, established in 1995, has facilitated over 18,500+ job offers to date, with 383 companies visiting campus in 2024-25 alone.
